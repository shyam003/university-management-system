Hostel Facilities

The University offers excellent living accommodation within the campus, separately for boys and Girls. The university understand expectations of students who come here from wide variety of cultural and geographical zones and perhaps leave their homes for the first time in their lives. The university ensures making this transition smooth and comfortable. The university has a wide range of options that. Meets the needs of all students. There are rooms ranging from Standard Rooms to Apartments and students can opt for accommodation as per their convenience and choice.

The hostels at LPU are basically of two types:

1. Standard Rooms:Standard Rooms at LPU hostels are arrangements like lodges where students can live in a shared 4/ 3/ 2/ 1-seater room with the option of having the room Air Cooled or Air-Conditioned Ventilation. The following amenities are provided to students of these rooms.

  • Iron Bed
  • Plastic Chair
  • Study Table
  • Almirah
  • Geyser
  • AC/Air Cooler

2. Appartments: The apartment accommodation at LPU are 1/ 2/ 3/ 4 rooms with attached washrooms. There are facilities constructed for providing luxurious accommodation. The apartment has a common kitchen area, dining and lobby area. Some other features of Apartments at LPU are given below.

  • Wooden Box-Bed
  • Plastic Chair
  • Study Table
  • Almirah
  • Geyser
  • AC

Wide range of Facilites in Hostels

  • Room with attached washroom bed chair table and almirah
  • Options of air-cooled or air-conditioned room
  • Separate mess facility in each hostel with vegetarian meals only
  • Apart from mess each hostel has a cafeteria which remains open 24/7 to facilitate the students who generally study till late night
  • Hygienic vegetarian meals with variety of cuisines (Indian, South Indian, Continental)
  • Free internet connectivity through Wi-Fi system (for academic purpose)
  • Hot water
  • Hospital (in campus) with resident doctors and ambulances
  • Counsellors for resolving issues like homesickness, Studies etc.
  • 24-hour security; Turnstile Access to residential areas for ensuring security
  • Elevator, ATM, Parking facility in each residential block
  • WH Smith departmental store for daily needs
  • Facilities of indoor and outdoor games Gymnasium(optional)
  • Laundry (optional)
  • 66 KVA substation for power supply and 24 hours power back-up through generators
  • Cloak Room facility for resident students
  • Online leave facility
  • Guest Room facility for Parents (subject to availability)
  • Elaborate fire safety arrangements as per norms
